This update is focused on making the app more intuitive, more powerful, and more helpful for everyday money tracking.

## Here’s what’s coming:

### 📊 New Dashboard Experience

A cleaner, more guided flow designed to improve retention and help users take action faster.

### 💸 Bill Splitting → Receivables

You’ll be able to split bills and record them directly as receivables, making it easier to track who owes you.

### 🏦 Transfer Vault → Receivables

Vault transfers can now be recorded as receivables too. This is useful for things like lending money to a friend and keeping it tracked properly.

### 📅 Calendar View

A new way to visualize your spending, streaks, quests, and bills in one simple calendar view.

### ⚙️ Bug Fixes & Performance Improvements

We’ve also fixed bugs and improved overall performance to make the app smoother and more reliable.

Thanks for being part of the journey. More details coming soon 👀
