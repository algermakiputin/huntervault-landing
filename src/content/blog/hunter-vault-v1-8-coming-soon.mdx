We're preparing the release of **Hunter Vault v1.8**, focused on improving progression, enhancing the dashboard experience, and introducing new customization features.

## Quest Fixes

We've resolved issues with quests that were previously impossible to complete. Progression will now be smoother and more consistent across all rank levels.

## New Quests

Additional quests have been introduced to create a more balanced and engaging progression system — with better rewards and improved pacing so every step forward feels earned.

## Profile Customization

You'll now be able to personalize your Hunter profile:

- Edit your username anytime
- Select your preferred avatar

## Dashboard & Rank Updates

The dashboard has been refined for better clarity at a glance. Rank badges have also been improved to better represent your current progress and how close you are to the next tier.
