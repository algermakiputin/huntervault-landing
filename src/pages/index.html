// Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Questions

Hunter Vault is a gamified personal finance tracker and budgeting app inspired by RPG games like Solo Leveling. It turns everyday financial habits into quests, rewards smart money decisions with XP and rank upgrades, and helps you track expenses, manage income, pay off debt, and reach savings goals — all 100% offline with no account required.

Hunter Vault turns financial actions into game mechanics. Every expense you track earns XP. Every debt payment advances your Hunter Rank. Savings goals become quests with progress bars. Instead of relying on willpower, gamification builds financial habits naturally — by making progress visible and every good decision feel rewarding.

Hunter Vault is free to download with core features available at no cost — expense tracking, budget management, quests, and rank progression are all included in the free tier. A premium upgrade is available for users who want to unlock the full experience. No account needed and your data always stays on your device.

Hunter Vault is 100% offline — your financial data never leaves your device and is never sent to any server. No accounts to create, no data collected, no cloud sync. Your privacy is protected by design, not just by policy.

Most budgeting apps feel like work — Hunter Vault feels like a game. Instead of spreadsheets and bar charts, you level up a Hunter character, complete daily quests, and rise through ranks from E to S+. It combines the discipline of a serious finance tracker with the motivation loop of an RPG, making it far easier to stay consistent with your money habits long-term.

Hunter Vault covers the full personal finance stack: expense tracking, income management, budget control (Mana Pool), debt payoff visualization (Raid system), savings goal tracking (Power Vaults), and a daily quest log that turns financial habits into missions. All in one app, no internet required.
